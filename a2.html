Goal/s

For the Townsville and Thuringowa Country Music Association's new website to be successful, it is crucial to increase user engagement on specific pages. The website incorporates various calls to action across multiple pages, encouraging users to engage by contacting the club, accessing monthly magazines, and exploring upcoming events. Several measurable goals can be assessed to determine if the new site achieves its objectives.

Firstly, the club typically hosts multiple events each month, with approximately one month between each type of event (e.g., general meetings on the third Wednesday of the month). Over a two-month period, the percentage of users visiting the events page and the number of people attending these events should be monitored. If the percentage of users accessing the events page is below 25% and event attendance hasn't increased by at least 10%, it indicates the need for improvements to entice users.

Another goal of the website is to track the number of users accessing the contact form. Within one month of the site's launch, the visitor count on this page should be assessed and if there hasn't been a minimum improvement of 20% in visitors, it implies that the calls to action on the homepage should be modified. Additionally, the placement and the design of the contact button in the header and footer may also have to be altered. Subsequently, another test can be conducted to evaluate the impact of these changes.
